Representative Jim Cooper

Representative for Tennessee’s 5th District

Cooper is the representative for Tennessee’s 5th congressional district and is a Democrat. He has served since Jan 7, 2003. Cooper is next up for reelection in 2022 and serves until Jan 3, 2023.
Jim Cooper sits on the following committees:
  • House Committee on Armed Services
  • House Committee on the Budget
  • House Committee on Oversight and Reform
